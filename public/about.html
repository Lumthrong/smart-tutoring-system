About Smart Tutoring

Smart Tutoring is an AI-powered learning platform designed to make studying easier, faster, and more organized. Students can access study materials, books, quizzes, and intelligent tools that help improve learning efficiency.

Study Resources

Access a wide range of academic books and learning materials organized by department and course.

AI Learning Tools

Generate quizzes and tests automatically using AI to help reinforce your understanding of study materials.

Smart Progress

Track your learning progress and improve your study performance using smart analytics.

Our Mission

The goal of Smart Tutoring is to create a modern digital learning environment where students can easily access knowledge and learn more efficiently using technology and AI.
